🍶 The Coveted Milk Run Award

The Legend

Every great tradition has an origin story. The Milk Run Award was born from one of those late-night moments that only happens on a golf trip — when somebody, somehow, ended up on a milk run at an hour that shall not be spoken of, in a condition that shall not be described, retrieving provisions that shall not be questioned.

Since that fateful night, the Milk Run Award has been presented each year to the individual who best exemplifies the spirit of the run: selfless service, questionable judgment, and an unwavering commitment to the crew — no matter the hour, no matter the cost.

It is not the most prestigious award on the trip. It may, however, be the most important.

📜 The Criteria

  • Must have gone above and beyond for the group — unsolicited
  • Bonus points for late-night heroics, food runs, or supply missions
  • Extra credit for doing so in a compromised state
  • Winner selected by group vote (or commissioner override)
